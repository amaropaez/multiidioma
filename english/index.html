Travel assistance


What is it and why do i need it?

More than travel insurance, it is travel assistance. The benefits of Regnitz River LLC Insurance will protect not only in the medical field, for example emergency dental care, for a sprain or fracture or discomfort in general, but also assistance of other kinds such as locating luggage, reimbursement of expenses for delayed or canceled flights, assistance in case of theft or loss of documents and much more. Travel with the peace of mind of being covered for any eventuality!

What do I do if I lose my documents?

The certificate of denunciation is necessary so that you can regularize your situation while abroad in order to request the new documents. Then go to the embassy of your country, they will help you with the printing of the new passport or obtain a safe-conduct that allows you to return to your country.
